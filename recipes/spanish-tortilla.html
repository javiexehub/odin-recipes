Spanish Tortilla



The first spanish-tortilla I made by my own :D

Ingredients (for two people)

  • 7 Potatoes
  • 9 eggs
  • 3 big onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Fry the potatoes untill they are quite soft
  2. add the onions near to the end of the potatoes cook process
  3. Add both of them to a ball with the nine 9 eggs previously mixed and seasoned with salt and pepper
  4. try the flavours, and act upon it
  5. put that mix in a hot pan with a little of oil and move it so it wont get stuck
  6. when you feel you have to turn it around, do it, use a plate to do so
  7. cook until you see great brown and goldish colours and smells
